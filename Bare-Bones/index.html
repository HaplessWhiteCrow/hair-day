Our story

Effortless Glam: A Step-by-Step Makeup Tutorial for a Night Out

At Hair Day, we believe that looking fabulous shouldn’t be complicated. Whether you’re prepping for a special event or just want to feel extra confident for a night out, we’ve got you covered. This easy-to-follow makeup tutorial will help you achieve a glamorous, radiant look in just a few steps—perfect for any occasion!

Step 1: Prep Your skin

Before applying any makeup, it’s important to prep your skin for a smooth, long-lasting finish.

  1. Start by cleansing your face to remove any impurities or oil.
  2. Apply a hydrating moisturizer to plump and smooth your skin.
  3. Finish with a primer to help your makeup glide on effortlessly and stay put all night long.

Pro Tip: hoose a primer that suits your skin type—mattifying for oily skin, or luminous for dry or dull skin.

Step 2: Create a Flawless Base

A glowing complexion is the foundation of any great makeup look.

  1. Apply a lightweight foundation that matches your skin tone using a damp beauty sponge or a brush. Blend well, starting from the center of your face and moving outward.
  2. Use a concealer under your eyes and on any blemishes or redness for extra coverage. Blend it out with your finger or sponge for a seamless finish.
  3. Set your base with a light dusting of translucent powder, especially in areas prone to oiliness like your T-zone.

Pro Tip: For a dewy look, skip the powder and opt for a cream-based highlighter instead.

Step 3: Define Your Eyes

The eyes are the focal point of this look, so let’s make them pop!

  1. Start by applying a neutral transition shade (a soft beige or light brown) to your crease using a fluffy blending brush. This will add depth and dimension to your eyes.
  2. Next, sweep a shimmery eyeshadow in a champagne or rose gold shade across your eyelids. This will give you that glowing, glam vibe.
  3. Use a darker shade, like a deep brown or plum, to softly define the outer corners of your eyes. Blend it well to avoid harsh lines.
  4. Line your upper lash line with a black or dark brown eyeliner, creating a subtle wing for a lifted effect.
  5. Finish with a generous coat of mascara to lengthen and volumize your lashes. You can also add a pair of false lashes for extra drama!

Pro Tip: For a bolder look, add a touch of shimmer or glitter to the center of your eyelids to catch the light!

Step 4: Add a Healthy Glow

Bring warmth and structure to your face with the perfect contour and highlight.

  1. Lightly dust bronzer along your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead for a sun-kissed glow.
  2. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks for a fresh, rosy finish.
  3. For a radiant, lit-from-within look, use a highlighter on the high points of your face: cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your cupid’s bow.

Pro tip: A cream highlighter gives a more natural, dewy finish, while powder highlighter delivers a bold, reflective glow.

Step 5: Perfect Your Lips

Finish off your glam look with a show-stopping lip.

  1. Start by lining your lips with a lip liner that matches your natural lip color to define their shape.
  2. Choose a bold lipstick shade that complements your look—deep red, berry, or nude tones work beautifully for a night out.
  3. Top it off with a swipe of gloss for extra shine and volume, or leave it matte for a more sophisticated finish.

Pro Tip: or long-lasting wear, apply a light layer of powder over your lips before adding the final coat of lipstick.

Step 6: Set It and Forget It

To keep your makeup looking flawless all night, finish with a spritz of setting spray. This will lock in your look, ensuring it stays put no matter where the night takes you!
